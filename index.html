About Us

Tumpat Solutions is a Malaysian company that provides consulting and engineering services for telecommunications systems. We have over 18 years of experience working with mobile operators, ISPs, equipment makers, power utilities, and the oil and gas industry. Our strengths are in wireless engineering, optical communications, and networking. We have worked on projects all over Malaysia, including Sabah and Sarawak.
